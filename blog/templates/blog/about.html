    My name is Spencer Tollefson. My current job is Peace Corps Volunteer in a small town in Costa Rica doing Community Economic Development. I’m digging into data science, coding, and other tangential fields head first. DISCLAIMER! I have no formal training in programming - at the end of 2017 I began self-teaching myself Python, listening to related podcasts, and digging into a whole bunch of different online resources in order to learn a thing or two.

    My goals with this blog are to jot down what I’ve been up to, what I’ve been learning, and use it as a diary in a sense. If you find yourself here, don't run away! Welcome! Feel free to reach out with advice, comments, questions, anything!

    More Information

    For my entire life my ~relationship~ with data sets had been as follows: I highly valued and was fascinated by the information and potential advice hidden within them, but never had learned the correct skills and tools to effectively analyze them. I am changing that relationship. Now. With the surge of interest in programming (notably Python) and data science as a field, the lack of tools to learn this stuff no longer serves as a valid excuse.

    Anyway, about me. Born and raised in the Seattle area, currently living in Costa Rica. To keep moving I dabble in body weight exercises as well as some basic cardio+podcasts. Craft beer is divine. Supporter of theSeahawks, Mariners, and NBA fan (RIP Sonics). I love the Crash Course YouTube channel. I read non-fiction and Wikipedia for pleasure. I find it difficult to focus on comedy TV shows – I could be learning something instead! Since mid-2017 I shifted my diet to eating mostly unprocessed plants. Worth it. Finally, I organize via maintaining Google Keep lists of nearly everything and I really like beer.
