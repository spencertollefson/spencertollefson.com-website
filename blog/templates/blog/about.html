About

My name is Spencer Tollefson. My current job is Peace Corps Volunteer in a small town in Costa Rica doing Community Economic Development. I’m digging into data science, coding, and other tangential fields head first. DISCLAIMER! I have no formal training in programming - at the end of 2017 I began self-teaching myself Python, listening to related podcasts, and digging into a whole bunch of different online resources in order to learn a thing or two.

My goals with this blog are to jot down what I’ve been up to, what I’ve been learning, and use it as a diary in a sense. If you find yourself here, don't run away! Welcome! Feel free to reach out with advice, comments, questions, anything!

More Information


I’ve had this ~relationship~ with data sets for a long time: I highly respected the information and actionable advice they may hold, but have not equipped myself with the tools to analyze them. I aim to change that. Now. With the surge of interest in programming (notably Python) and data science as a field, the lack of tools to learn this stuff no longer serves as a valid excuse.

Anyway, about me. Born and raised in the Seattle area, currently living in Costa Rica. I like staying fit via body weight exercises as well as some basic cardio + podcasts. I’m a Seahawks, Mariners, and NBA fan (RIP Sonics). I love the Crash Course YouTube channel. I read non-fiction and Wikipedia for pleasure. I find it difficult to focus on comedy TV shows – I could be learning something instead! Since mid-2017 Finally, I eat a lot of plants, keep Google Keep lists of nearly everything and really like beer.
